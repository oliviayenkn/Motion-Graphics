MOTION GRAPHICS

Introduction

Motion graphics is a kind of animation that focuses on providing motion to graphic design elements. It is a way of adding depth to design elements and to highlight the message, where graphic design can be seen more static. Motion graphics can help to provide a better user experience when for example entering the wrong password. Motion graphics dates back to around the 19th century where a German filmmaker, Walter Ruttman, was the first director to employ visualization techniques like motion graphics.

TEXT IN FOCUS

Motion graphics is animation with a strong focus on text, also called animation graphic design.

MOTION GRAPHICS vs. ANIMATION

Any technique that makes static objects move is basically animation. Motion graphic separates from other kinds of animation because of its content which is setting graphic design (that being objects, text or other elements) in motion, where animation itself focuses more on a cinematic effect and storytelling.

STRONG COMMUNICATOR

Motion graphics are a great way to communicate with the viewer as moving objects instantly draw attention. With motion graphics you can illustrate your message in a fun and engaging way for the viewer.

AUDIO

Motion graphic often makes use of audio to highlight a message for example voice-over or music. Audio can be used to build a relationship with the viewer or establish a mood or a narrative.

SOFTWARE

After effects is a software programme that allows you to animate 2D. It is easy to use together with other Adobe programmes like Illustrator and Photoshop. This is great because you would normally use several programmes in order to make your motion graphic. A typical workflow could be to start designing your assets in illustrator, then importing them into After Effects and adding visual effects. After effects also has a lot of different plugins that makes the platform even more versatile.

PEN TOOL

The pen tool allows you to create any kind of shape. The pen tool can be used to mask out areas or draw a shape layer. It also allows you to draw and adjust paths, which is the guidance for a layer to move along.

GRAPH EDITOR

The graph editor makes it possible to adjust the motion curves of your keyframes in order to highlight the message that you wanna communicate.

SHAPE LAYERS

Shape layers are 2D objects. You can create shape layers with the peen tool or predetermined shapes. Shape layers make it possible to customise each properties such as stroke, fill or corner roundness.

TRACK MATTE

Track matte uses one layer to mask another layer. The track matte “cuts” a hole in a layer to expose the layer below.

BRANDING

Nowadays, video is the must used and best way to communicate to people. More media platforms support videos, and consumers prefer watching brand video than reading the same information, because this media creates engagement and builds strong brand loyalty. Motion graphics are a great way to strengthen your brand identity.

MOVEMENT

The motion graphics need to reflect the brand as motion contributes to recognizability. You are creating a brand’s body language, so think about what identity to want to communicate with movement.

LOGO

Motion graphics are perfect for logo animations. Moving objects instantly draw attention, so it’s a great way to get people to remember your brand name and logo.

INFORMATION

Motion graphics are a great tool to convey information in an easy-understanding matter. Motion graphic get straight to the point with very basic visual elements.

PRODUCT

Depending on your product, motion graphics are a great way to help your product stand out. Even the less appealing product can become attractive by using motion graphics the right way.
